1. String Conversion

2. Numeric Conversion
   - Implicit in math operations, e.g., `"6"/"2" = 3`.

3. Boolean Conversion
   - Use `Boolean(value)` or contexts like `if`, `!`, `&&`, `||`.
   - Falsy: `0`, `null`, `undefined`, `NaN`, `""` â†’ `false`.
   - Everything else (even `"0"` or `" "`) â†’ `true`.

4. Summary Table
   - String: `String(value)`
   - Number: `Number(value)` (or `+value`)
   - Boolean: `Boolean(value)` (or `!!value`)

5. Gotchas to Remember
   - `undefined` â†’ `NaN`, *not* `0`.
   - `"0"` and whitespace strings â†’ `true` in boolean context.
   - Math operations auto-convert; mixing types may lead to unexpected results.
